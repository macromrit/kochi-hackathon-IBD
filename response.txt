\section*{Key Recommendations}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Keep a food diary to track foods eaten and note when symptoms worsen to identify problematic foods.
    \item Eat small, frequent meals and avoid skipping meals.
    \item Avoid fatty, fried foods, spicy foods, dairy (except curd), and high-fiber foods.
    \item Stay hydrated and focus on low-fiber, easily digestible foods.
    \item Include probiotics such as curd to maintain gut health.
    \item Avoid gluten-containing foods.
    \item Follow a high-calorie, high-protein diet to prevent malnutrition.
    \item Avoid unhealthy fats; opt for healthy fats like coconut oil, olive oil, and small fish.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Monitoring and Adjustments}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Regularly monitor symptoms and adjust the diet based on tolerance and response.
    \item Consult with a healthcare provider for ongoing support and adjustments.
\end{itemize}
